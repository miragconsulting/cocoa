About EMEN CAPITAL

EMEN CAPITAL SPÓŁKA Z OGRANICZONĄ ODPOWIEDZIALNOŚCIĄ is a leading European distributor of premium cocoa products from Venezuela, specializing in the finest Fino de Aroma and Criollo varieties.

In addition to our premium cocoa range, we are proud to be the official distributor of JAR flavorings - a renowned Polish company with over 45 years of experience in developing exceptional flavoring solutions for the food industry.

With our extensive logistics network spanning across Slovenia, Poland, Hungary, Germany, and Russia, we ensure timely and efficient delivery of our products to clients throughout Europe.

Our Premium Products

Premium Cocoa Products

Our cocoa products are sourced from the finest Venezuelan plantations, specializing in Fino de Aroma and Criollo varieties - known for their exceptional aroma and taste profiles.

Cocoa Beans

Premium raw, roasted, fermented/non-fermented beans from Venezuela's finest plantations.

Product Specifications:
  • Raw beans - natural flavor profile
  • Roasted beans - enhanced chocolate notes
  • Fermented/non-fermented options
  • Fino de Aroma & Criollo varieties
  • Packaging: 25kg bags or custom options

Perfect for chocolate makers seeking the highest quality raw materials for premium chocolate production.

Cocoa Nibs

Crushed, cleaned cocoa beans perfect for chocolate, baking, granola, and more.

Product Specifications:
  • 100% pure crushed cocoa beans
  • No additives or preservatives
  • Available in various granulation sizes
  • Rich in antioxidants and minerals
  • Packaging: 5kg, 10kg, 25kg options

Ideal for industrial bakeries, chocolate manufacturers, and health food producers.

Cocoa Butter

Premium pressed cocoa butter for chocolate production, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals.

Product Specifications:
  • 100% pure pressed cocoa butter
  • Food-grade quality
  • Melting point: 34-38°C
  • Free fatty acids: max 1.75%
  • Packaging: 25kg blocks or custom options

Essential for premium chocolate production, cosmetics, and pharmaceutical applications.

Cocoa Powder

Natural and alkalized cocoa powder options with exceptional flavor profiles.

Product Specifications:
  • Natural (pH 5-6) and alkalized (Dutch process) options
  • Fat content: 10-12%, 20-22% options
  • Fineness: 99.5% through 200 mesh
  • Rich brown color with complex flavor profile
  • Packaging: 25kg bags

Perfect for bakeries, ice cream manufacturers, and beverage producers seeking premium cocoa flavoring.

Cocoa Mass (Liquor)

Ground cocoa beans forming a semi-solid paste - the foundation of chocolate making.

Product Specifications:
  • 100% pure ground cocoa beans
  • Fat content: 52-55%
  • Available in solid blocks or liquid form
  • Intense chocolate flavor profile
  • Packaging: 25kg blocks or custom options

The essential base for all chocolate manufacturing processes, offering rich flavor and smooth texture.

Cocoa Shells (Husk)

By-product of cocoa processing used for animal feed, fertilizer, tea production, and cosmetics.

Product Specifications:
  • Cleaned and processed cocoa shells
  • Rich in fiber and minerals
  • Available in various granulation sizes
  • Suitable for multiple applications
  • Packaging: 20kg bags

Sustainable by-product with applications in agriculture, tea production, and eco-friendly packaging.

Food Colors
